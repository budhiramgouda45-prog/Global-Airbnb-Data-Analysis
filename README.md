# Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
I developed a Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard using Power BI to analyze Airbnb listing data across different locations and solve key business problems.

---

## Business Problem
Stakeholders did not have a clear understanding of:
- Pricing trends  
- Customer preferences  
- Overall business performance  

To solve this, I collected and cleaned the dataset in Excel by handling missing values and ensuring data consistency. Then, I imported the data into Power BI and performed data modeling to establish relationships between tables.

---

## Solution
- Built an interactive dashboard using Power BI  
- Visualized key metrics such as:
  - Total Listings  
  - Total Cities  
  - Total Hosts  
  - Property Types  
  - Reviews  
- Created visualizations for:
  - Location-wise performance  
  - Room type distribution  
  - Pricing trends over time  
  - City-wise ratings (accuracy, cleanliness, communication, location, value)  

---

## Key Insights

- Total Listings: **279,712**, Cities: **10**, Hosts: **182,024**, Property Types: **144**  

- Airbnb business started in **2008** and grew steadily from **2011 to 2015**, reaching peak growth in 2015.  
  A decline was observed during **2016–2017**, likely due to stricter regulations.  
  The business recovered between **2017–2019**, but growth slowed in **2019 due to COVID-19**.  

- **Paris, New York, and Sydney** contribute nearly **50% of total listings** and **48% of total reviews**.  
  Paris has the highest listings and reviews, possibly due to higher hotel prices compared to Airbnb.  

- **Mexico City and Rio** have higher overall ratings, while **Hong Kong and Istanbul** show lower ratings.  
  Cleanliness and value-for-money are the lowest-rated factors across cities.  

- More than **two-thirds of Airbnb hosts are fully verified**, and most hosts provide at least one trust signal.  
  Unverified and anonymous profiles are minimal.  

---

## Business Impact

- Helps stakeholders identify **high-performing cities** for investment  
- Supports **pricing strategy optimization** based on location trends  
- Improves understanding of **customer preferences by room type**  
- Highlights **areas of improvement** such as cleanliness and value  
- Builds trust insights by analyzing **host verification patterns**
